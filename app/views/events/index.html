What is The Sipping Point?

Worried no one will show up to your party? Set The Sipping Point.

1. Pick a Time & Place
2. Set a mininum number of guests (The Sipping Point)
3. Invite your friends

If enough people RSVP before the deadline, then it's on!

If the deadline passes without reaching The Sipping Point, the event is automatically canceled. No worries.
